Available Services


     At Floyd's Body Shop we have more than four decades of experience. As a family owned company that was founded with strong values, we believe in offering only top-quality services to you as our customer.

Full-Service Auto Body Repair: Our body repair service covers all makes and models, from the front end to the rear end. Upon completion, you will not be able to tell the car was damaged.

Complete Paint Services: Regardless of the current color or state of your cars paint, with the use of current color matching technologies, your car may look even better than it did before!

Restoration: Bring your car back to life, or keep your hot rod in mint condition with our restoration and repair services. This includes - but not limited to - Metal Repair, Fiberglass Repair, Refinishing, and assembly.

Glass Repair: Whether you need a windshield, side mirrors or door windows fixed, Floyd's Body Shop can meet all of your automotive glass needs.

PDR: (Paintless Dent Repair) is available for dings and dents such as hail damage, where the paint is not chipped or cracked.

ETC: If you have questions about any of our services available, feel free to call during our business hours, and we will work with you to achieve a timely and cost effective solution to your project.
